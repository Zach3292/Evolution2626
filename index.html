Our Story

It all began during the 2006-2007 school year, when Mr. Laurent Beaudoin from Bombardier came to the Séminaire de Sherbrooke to invite us to participate in a well-known robotics competition held in the United States and in English Canada, FRC for FIRST Robotics Competition.

A small group of students and teachers went to Boston to attend a FRC competition.

It turns out that the next year, a new team will participate in the great competition FIRST, it will be the 2626 Evolution team. Since 2008, the team has been improving every year.
